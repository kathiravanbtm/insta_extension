# Privacy Policy for MaxiReel

**Last Updated**: November 13, 2025

## Overview
MaxiReel is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our extension handles your data.

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.**

### What We Access
The extension requires the following permissions to function:

1. **activeTab**: To access and enhance videos on Instagram pages you visit
2. **storage**: To save your extension settings locally on your device
3. **downloads**: To allow you to download Instagram videos (optional)
4. **menus**: To add context menu items for quick actions
5. **https://www.instagram.com/***: To run only on Instagram pages

### How We Use Permissions

- **activeTab & Instagram Access**: Used solely to detect videos on Instagram and add enhancement controls. No data is sent anywhere.
- **storage**: Settings are stored locally on your browser using `browser.storage.sync`. This data stays on your device and is synced across your Firefox account if you use Firefox Sync.
- **downloads**: Only used when you explicitly click the download button. Videos are downloaded directly from Instagram's servers to your computer.
- **menus**: Creates right-click context menu options for quick access to features.

## Data Storage
- All settings are stored locally in your browser
- No data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking is performed
- No cookies are created or used

## Third-Party Services
This extension does NOT:
- Use any third-party analytics services
- Send data to external servers
- Track your browsing activity
- Store any information about the videos you watch

## Video Downloads
When you use the download feature:
- Videos are downloaded directly from Instagram's servers
- We do not intercept, store, or modify the video content
- Downloads are handled entirely by your browser
- No download history is kept by the extension

## Data Security
Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure or breach. All extension functionality operates locally in your browser.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Your Rights
You have complete control over the extension:
- You can disable or remove the extension at any time
- You can revoke any permissions through Firefox settings
- You can clear stored settings by removing the extension

## Data Retention
- Settings are retained only as long as the extension is installed
- Removing the extension deletes all stored settings
- No data is retained after uninstallation

## Children's Privacy
This extension does not specifically target children and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.
