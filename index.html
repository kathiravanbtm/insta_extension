Instagram Video Enhancer

Professional video controls and enhancements for Instagram

About

A lightweight Chrome and Firefox extension that enhances your Instagram video experience with advanced controls, keyboard shortcuts, and a responsive interface that adapts to any video size.

Controls are hidden by default for a clean viewing experience. Press Y to toggle visibility, or simply move your mouse in fullscreen mode.

Installation

Chrome

  1. Visit Chrome Web Store
  2. Search for "Instagram Video Enhancer"
  3. Click "Add to Chrome"
  4. Visit Instagram and enjoy!

Firefox

  1. Visit Firefox Add-ons
  2. Search for "Instagram Video Enhancer"
  3. Click "Add to Firefox"
  4. Visit Instagram and enjoy!

Pro Tips

  • Press Y anytime to show/hide controls
  • Move your mouse in fullscreen to reveal controls temporarily
  • Use J and K for quick zoom adjustments
  • Press M to mute/unmute without showing controls
  • All Instagram UI is hidden in fullscreen for distraction-free viewing
